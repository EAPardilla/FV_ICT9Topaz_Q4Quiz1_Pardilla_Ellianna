Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
  • He is part of a rich family and is originally
    from the town of San Diego.
  • Was sent to Europe to continue his education there.
  • He came back to the Philippines after hearing
    about his father's death.
Maria Clara
  • Ibarra's one and only fiance
  • Daughter of Kapitan Tiago
  • A sweet, kind, and humble woman loved by everyone.
  • While Ibarra was in Europe, she was sent to study
    in the Beaterio de Santa Clara.
Kapitan Tiago
  • Maria Clara's father
  • His wife, Pia Alba, died after giving birth to
    their daughter
  • A rich and influential businessman
Sisa
  • The loving mother of Crispin and Basilio
  • Lost her sanity after the loss of her two sons and
    expreiencing trauma from her abusive husband
